Doctor Stego's Favorite Champions

Learn more about some of Doctor Stego's favorite champions here!

Annie - Pyromaniac

Q - Hurls a fireball at a target
W - Blasts a cone of fire
E - Gains movement speed and armor/magic resist
R - Summons her teddy bear, Tibbers, to attack enemies

  • Has a ton of pressure and control
  • Basically an easy cc hit
  • Every 4 spells, her next ability stuns
  • Most effective when combined with her ultimate
  • Has a monster bear that kills things

Kayle - The Righteous

Q - Launches a powerful celestial sword
W - Heals Kayle and another nearby ally
E - Enhances Kayle's next auto attack, dealing % missing health
R - Makes an allied target invulnerable

  • Becomes a god late game
  • E & R don't cost mana

Illaoi - Kraken Goddess

Q - Slams a tentacle in a straight line
W - Leaps to a target, damaging it, and causes surround tentacles to slam
E - Rips out a soul of another champion, to be damaged
R - Slams onto the floor causing all surround tentacles to hit faster

  • Powerful when getting ganked
  • With good positioning, can easily get a pentakill
  • Great when dealing with tanks
